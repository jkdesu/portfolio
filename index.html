Ji-in Kang is an aspiring brand designer with a background in Economics from Waseda University in Tokyo. She is currently pursuing a master's degree in Computational Design Practices at Columbia University, where she explores the intersection of culture and emotion through visual identity. Using computational tools, she adds conceptual layers to her design language—often drawing from frameworks like Venn diagrams to map relationships. Data visualization plays a key role in her creative process, helping her communicate complex ideas in engaging and meaningful ways.
